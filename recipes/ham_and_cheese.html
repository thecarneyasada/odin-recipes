Ooey Gooey Ham and Cheese

Description

The best ham and cheese sandwich on the face of the planet

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Lightly Toast Bread
  3. Assemble Ham and Cheese
  4. Place Ham and Cheese on skillet and cook on med heat until cheese is melted
  5. When cheese is melted, apply pickles and mayo
